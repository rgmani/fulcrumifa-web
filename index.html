Who we work with

The largest marginal
impact potential.

We focus on people whose financial lives could be transformed by one clear conversation. Not exclusively wealthy clients — the people financial services routinely passes over.

Tradespeople

Self-employed & independent

Builders, electricians, plumbers — high earning, no employer pension, no one explaining the VAT threshold trap or the cost of leaving retirement unplanned.

Young professionals

Early career, long runway

Nurses, engineers, developers — employer match going unclaimed, ISA discipline not yet built, pension default fund quietly underperforming for years.

Underserved communities

Historically overlooked

Ethnic minority communities in the UK are significantly less likely to hold investments or have a pension above minimum levels. Language, trust, and access are all barriers we aim to reduce.
